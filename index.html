The Eye Love Story

Born from the love of my craft and customers, Eye Love Optical Boutique is the realization of a dream. A dream to provide unique, beautiful, high-quality eyewear to my customers. A dream of helping people overcome vision challenges and making sure they look incredible in their new eyewear. A dream of supporting independent eyewear manufacturers and supporting causes that are important to me.

Eye Love Optical Boutique realizes that everyone is amazing in their own way, and we work hard to provide eyewear as unique and as beautiful as you.

Bevel

Bevel is committed to making eyewear as essential to personal style and self-esteem as apparel or cosmetics.

Dutz

The Dutz collection targets men & women of all ages. Dutz has paid special attention to those with large and small faces who have difficulty finding a frame that fits.

Etnia Barcelona

Etnia Barcelona is color, quality, culture. They are Mediterranean, independent and a rebel. They are the city that inspires them: Barcelona.

Face a Face

Far from standardized fashion, each collection asserts its artistic temperament, drawing its inspiration from the sources of Modern Art, architecture and contemporary design

State

STATE Optical Co. is a first-of-its-kind brand of luxury American eyewear built from the ground up in Chicago. State frames are meticulously handcrafted to be a genuine expression of one's personal style and distinct personality.

American Optical

A true original since 1833. The oldest and greatest optical company in American history has pioneered optical and eyewear innovation for centuries.

Maui Jim

Maui Jim got its start in 1980 as a small company selling sunglasses on the beach in Lahaina, Hawaii. Now a market leader, their revolutionary PolarizedPlus2® lens combat intense glare and harmful UV while bringing the brilliant colors to life.
